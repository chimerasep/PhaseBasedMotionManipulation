How It Works

Phase Analysis

Our system analyzes motion sequences by breaking them down into distinct phases, allowing for precise manipulation and control of movement patterns.

Motion Control

By understanding the phase relationships in motion, we can modify and control movement patterns while maintaining natural motion characteristics.

Implementation

Our implementation combines advanced algorithms with real-time processing to achieve smooth and natural motion manipulation.
